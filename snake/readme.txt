Snake
+++++

V1.0 24/11/03
V1.1 26/11/03 - Added statistics to game over screen

Requires Directx 9 and darkbasic pro to compile.

Onscreen

Size = total num of snake segments on screen
Eaten = total num of food items eaten
Play time = length of game
Ave = average length of time between eats, only updated on eats.
Dist = distance in snake segments from food
A = approx length of time spend going away from food
Best = longest snake you have grown (stored in score.txt, delete to reset).

Game over screen

All = total num of food items eaten
1/1 = total num of full food items eated
3/4 = total num of three quarter food items eaten
1/2 = total num of half food items eaten
1/4 = total num of one quarter food items eaten
0/0 = total num of food items that perished into nothing

Play time = total length of game
AVer time = average length of time between eats
